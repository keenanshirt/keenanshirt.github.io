To add the signature in Outlook:

Step 1: Hover over your choice and click to select all the elements in the signature. 

Step 2: Copy (Ctrl + C) the selection

Step 3: Open an Outlook email message and Paste (Ctrl +V) the signature in the email body. 

Step 4: Personalize your signature! Delete rows that you don’t want, or which don’t apply.

·       If needed, the Arts Fest logo was sent to you by email.

o   You may have to resize the image in the “Edit signature” box—If you can’t drag in from the corner to resize, right-click on the image, select “Picture...” and navigate to the Size tab. Under Scale, keep ‘Lock aspect ratio’ and ‘Relative to original picture size’ checked, then reduce the height/width percentage until you are satisfied.

Step 5: Select all the elements of the signature, right-click and choose Copy. 

Step 6: In the Insert tab, select Signature and choose Signatures from the drop-down menu. 

Step 7: Choose New and type a name for your signature. For example: "Arts Fest 2025". 

Step 8: In the Edit signature field, right-click and select Paste. Your signature is now displayed in the field. 

Step 9: Just below the edit signature field select Save.
